🛠️ How to Use FBCloner Tool in Kali Linux
FBCloner is a phishing-based Facebook account cloning tool. It is used to create fake login pages that look like Facebook, capturing credentials entered by the target.

⚠️ Warning: This tool is for educational and ethical penetration testing purposes only. Unauthorized use is illegal.
